Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life

Authors: Héctor García and Francesc Miralles

Only staying active will make you want to live a hundred years. — Japanese Proverb

About the Book

This international bestseller introduces readers to the Japanese concept of Ikigai — the reason for being. Discover the secrets of the long-living residents of Okinawa, Japan, and how practices like mindfulness, healthy eating, strong social ties, and finding purpose in life contribute to a long, happy life.

  • Lessons from Japan’s centenarians
  • The importance of flow and purpose

About the Authors

Héctor García is a software engineer and writer who has lived in Japan for over a decade. Francesc Miralles is a bestselling author and journalist. Together, they bring a blend of cultural insight and psychological wisdom to the topic of purposeful living.
